Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information kiwi ("kiwi", "we", "us", or "our") collects when you use our website, how that information is used, and the choices you have. By using kiwi, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

kiwi is a free, ad-supported website that lets you play browser games instantly, without downloads or accounts. We do not ask you to register or sign in, and we do not sell personal information.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

kiwi does not require an account, so we do not ask for your name, address, or payment details. If you email us (for example a support or DMCA request), we receive whatever information you choose to include in that message.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our servers and analytics tools may automatically record standard technical information, such as your IP address, browser type and language, device and operating system, referring pages, and the dates and times of your visits. This helps us keep the site running, understand which games are popular, and prevent abuse.

Information stored in your browser

kiwi uses your browser's local storage to remember preferences such as your favorite games, recently played games, and sound settings. This information stays on your device, is not transmitted to us, and can be cleared at any time from your browser settings.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device. We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies (such as web beacons and local storage) to operate the site, remember preferences, measure traffic, and deliver and measure advertising.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some features work.

4. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use kiwi so we can improve it. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect aggregated, largely anonymized usage data. You can learn more from Google's Privacy Policy, and you can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Advertising

kiwi is supported by advertising. We may work with third-party advertising partners, including Google, to show ads on our site.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to kiwi and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out of a third party's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info and Your Online Choices.

Third-party advertising partners have their own privacy policies governing how they collect and use data. We encourage you to review them. For more information on how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

6. Third-party games and links

Games and other content on kiwi may be loaded from or link to third-party services. Those third parties may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of websites or services we do not operate.

7. Children's privacy

kiwi is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

8. Your choices

  • Adjust or block cookies through your browser settings.
  • Opt out of personalized ads using the links in the Advertising section above.
  • Clear local storage (favorites, recent games, settings) from your browser at any time.
  • Use the Google Analytics opt-out add-on to stop analytics measurement.

9. Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited information we handle. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Significant changes will be reflected on this page, and your continued use of kiwi after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at kiwi.tk@mail.com or through our contact page.
