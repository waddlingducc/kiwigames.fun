Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

kiwi is a free site for playing browser games. There isn't a lot to say here, honestly — we don't collect much, because there's not much we need. Here's the short version.

No accounts

You don't sign up to play, so we never ask for your name, email, or anything personal. If you email us yourself — say, about a takedown — we'll obviously see whatever you put in that message.

What your browser saves

Your favorite games, what you've played recently, and your sound settings get saved right in your browser. That stays on your device, never gets sent to us, and you can wipe it any time by clearing your browser data.

Basic logs

Like every website, our server keeps standard logs — your IP address, which browser you're on, the pages you hit. We use them to keep the site up and stop abuse. Nothing beyond that.

Cookies

We keep cookies to a minimum and only use them to make the site work. You can block or delete them in your browser settings, but a few things might act up if you do.

Other people's games and links

A lot of what you play here is loaded from other sites, and we link out to places we don't run. Once you're dealing with them, you're under their rules, not ours — we can't control how they handle your data.

Kids

kiwi isn't built for children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect anything from them. If you think a kid sent us something personal, email us and we'll delete it.

Changes

If we change this page, we'll update the date up top. That's it.

Contact

Questions? Email kiwi.tk@mail.com or use our contact page.
