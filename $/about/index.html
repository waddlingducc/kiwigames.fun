About kiwi

Free browser games, ready when you are.

kiwi is a free online games portal. We bring together a hand-picked collection of browser games so you can jump straight into playing — no downloads, no installs, and no accounts required.

What we do

Our goal is simple: make it effortless to find a fun game and start playing in seconds. Every game on kiwi runs directly in your web browser, so whether you have a few minutes or a few hours, there is always something ready to play on desktop or mobile.

Instant play

Click a game and it loads right in your browser. No waiting for downloads or installs.

No account needed

You never have to sign up or log in. Your favorites and recent games are saved privately in your browser.

Hand-picked variety

From racing and action to puzzles and arcade classics, our library covers a wide range of tastes.

Works everywhere

kiwi is designed to work smoothly across modern browsers and screen sizes.

How it works

Browse the homepage or use the search bar to find a game by name. Click it to open its page and start playing. Tap the star on any game to save it to your favorites, and your recently played games appear automatically so you can pick up where you left off.

Respecting creators

Games featured on kiwi are the property of their respective creators and owners. We respect intellectual property rights and respond to valid removal requests. If you are a rights holder, please see our DMCA policy.

Get in touch

Have feedback, a question, or a request? We'd love to hear from you — visit our contact page or email kiwi.tk@mail.com.
