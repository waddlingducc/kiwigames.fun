Contact us

We usually reply within a few business days.

Questions, feedback, or a request? We're happy to hear from you. The best way to reach the kiwi team is by email.

Email us at:

kiwi.tk@mail.com

What to contact us about

  • General questions and feedback — ideas, suggestions, or anything about the site.
  • Bug reports — if a game or page isn't working, tell us what happened and which browser you're using.
  • Privacy questions — anything about our Privacy Policy or your data choices.
  • Copyright and removals — rights holders can request removal of content. Please review our DMCA policy first so your notice includes everything we need to act quickly.

Tips for a faster reply

When you email us, please include as much detail as you can — for example the name of the game, a link to the page, your browser and device, and a short description of what you were doing. The more we know, the faster we can help.
